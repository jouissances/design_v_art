Michael Giacchino’s Up (Married Life)

Michael Giacchino is an American composer of music for films, television and video games. Giacchino has received an Emmy Award and multiple Grammy Awards, as well as a Golden Globe Award and an Academy Award for his work on Up. [Wikipedia]


  • Love. Joy and sadness. Reminiscence.
  • As the song plays during the opening scene, it chronicles the life of the main character Carl and his wife Ellie, showing vignettes of their happy moments, as well as their sad moments. During the more joyful moments in the scene, we can see the theme transforms to become slightly more upbeat and cheerful, and accordingly, the song takes on a mournful tone during the grieving scene. Throughout the entire song, however, despite the ups-and-downs, it retains a consistent theme from start to finish, reflecting that despite of all the changes that the couple went through, their love has always been consistent.
  • As the opening scene had no dialogue, viewers had to rely on the soundtrack to derive meaning from the interactions between the two portrayed characters. It has done so successfully.
